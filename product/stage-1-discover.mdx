## What the customer sees

An escort discovers Exotic through one of three channels — a referral from a friend or colleague already on the platform, an outbound call from a member of the Exotic sales team, or an organic Google search for escort listing sites. In all cases, their first impression is either the public Exotic website or a direct conversation with a sales agent.

Referrals are the highest-converting channel: an escort who hears "I'm on Exotic and I'm getting good clients" from a trusted colleague arrives with strong intent. Outbound calls from agents are the highest-volume channel. Organic SEO arrivals are self-service and require the least sales effort but may need guidance through registration.

---

## Under the hood

When a prospective escort is identified by the sales team, they are created as a **Lead** in the CRM.

| What happens | Code reference |
|---|---|
| Lead created manually by sales agent | `LeadController` — `app/Http/Controllers/CRM/LeadController.php` |
| Lead imported via CSV or web scraper | `app/Services/SupportBoardLeadImportService.php`; Scraper sources in Settings |
| Lead record fields | `app/Models/Lead.php` — stores name, phone (normalised), email, source, status, assigned agent |
| Lead status lifecycle | `new` → `contacted` → `qualified` → `converted` → `lost` |
| Phone normalised automatically | Local format (e.g. `0712...`) → international format (e.g. `254712...`) |
