## What the customer sees

As an escort's subscription approaches expiry, they begin receiving SMS or WhatsApp reminder messages from Exotic. These messages are sent on a cadence configured by the sales team (e.g. 14 days before, 7 days before, 3 days before, day of expiry).

The message typically says something like:

> *"Your Exotic listing expires in 7 days. Renew now to keep your profile live and continue receiving clients. Reply or call us on [number]."*

If the escort doesn't renew, their profile goes private on expiry and they stop receiving client enquiries. This is the primary driver for renewal — the direct revenue impact.

## Under the hood

| What happens | Code reference |
|---|---|
| Renewal overview (buckets: active, risk, pending, expired, lapsed) | `RenewalService::buildOverview()` — `app/Services/RenewalService.php:38` |
| Running a renewal campaign | `RenewalController::run()` — `app/Http/Controllers/CRM/RenewalController.php:23` — dispatches SMS/email reminders |
| Campaign configuration | `RenewalCampaign` model — trigger days, product, template, channel |
| Run history | `RenewalRun` model — records every execution with targets reached, sent, failed, skipped |
| Expiry detection | `deals.expires_at`; legacy: `clients.escort_expire`, `clients.premium_expire`, `clients.featured_expire` |
