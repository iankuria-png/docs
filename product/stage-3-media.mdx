## What the customer sees

After registration, the escort logs into their WordPress account and fills in their profile. This includes their bio, rates, city, available services, and contact preferences. They then upload photos and — optionally — videos. The quality and completeness of this step directly affects how many enquiries they receive once the profile goes live.

Photos are uploaded through the WordPress profile editor. Up to **20 images** (max 5MB each) and **5 videos** are supported. Accepted formats: JPEG, PNG, WebP, GIF, AVIF. One photo must be designated as the main (cover) image — this appears in search result cards.

---

## Under the hood

| What happens | Code reference |
|---|---|
| Photo upload handled by WordPress plugin | `class-media-endpoint.php` in `exotic-crm-sync` — endpoints: `GET/POST/DELETE /clients/{post_id}/media` |
| Upload limits | 5MB per image, 20 images max, 5 videos max (configurable via WP options) |
| CRM display image selection | `ClientProfileImageService::normalise()` — `app/Services/ClientProfileImageService.php:10` |
| Non-blocking upload queue | Recent commits made uploads non-blocking + added retry controls — slow uploads don't block form submission |
| Profile completeness score | Computed from weighted profile fields; shown in CRM → client detail → Overview tab |
