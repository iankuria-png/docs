## Get started in 3 steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Navigate to your CRM URL and sign in with your email and password. Admins can also enable Google SSO in Settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your market">
    Go to **Settings → Integrations** and add your WordPress advertising platform. The CRM will sync existing client profiles automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start working">
    Open **Clients** to view synced profiles, **Leads** to begin your pipeline, or **Dashboard** to see live revenue and renewal workload.
  </Step>
</Steps>
